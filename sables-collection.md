# Sables

### TEEORA — Autumn / Winter 2026

**Collection description**
Sables is TEEORA's Autumn/Winter 2026 wardrobe — a study in warm neutrals, natural fibres and quiet construction. Twenty pieces, from bias-silk eveningwear to double-faced outerwear, each reduced to its essential line and finished by hand, and made to be worn for years rather than seasons.

**Intro**
Named for the shifting light of the desert at dusk, *Sables* gathers the season into a single considered palette — oyster and bone, camel and sage, espresso and ink. Nothing shouts. Every piece is cut to layer, to travel and to endure, relying on cloth, proportion and the confidence to leave things unadorned. This is luxury measured not in logos, but in the fall of a hem and the weight of a sleeve.
